About us

We, TREASURE STAR PTE.LTD. (Registration No.201709203W), are a global consultancy firm specialising in industry, strategy and management consultation.

What we do?

We advise clients and guide them through their most problematic business issues across the entire spectrum; from organisational effectiveness to strategy/management to digital transformation. We are passionate and determined about what we do to make an impact on society, industries and our clients; via our thought leadership and pragmatic solutions. Our unparalleled world-class expertise will enable businesses around the globe to enhance performance and outcome, which is tailored to each organisation’s unique situation.

We create value by crossing boundaries, together.

Established on 3rd April 2017, we have assisted clients to build the best management teams with the brightest minds in the world. Our unique perspectives and values have enabled us to align our incentive with the client’s ambitions and objectives so that all parties involved know that we are together in this - We care for our client’s business as our own. We collaborate with clients to create effective business solutions and insights that not only allow them to thrive in a competitive landscape, but also expand and capture new markets. Our focus on practical actions and strategic decisions are custom-tailored and not off-the-shelf solutions.
